THE MONGOLIAN FILM COLLECTIVE

ABOUT US

The Mongolian Film Collective is an independent organization spearheaded by filmmaker Cameron James.   

Founded in 2018 with no ties to the nation of Mongolia, MFC has released over two dozen independent film projects and student videos on a YouTube channel that boasts over 200 subscribers.
